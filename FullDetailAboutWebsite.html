Question and Answer

 Can a student jump into Backend without the knowledge of Frontend?

Yes,  student can  move directly to backend without the knowledge of frontend. But at this case our professioners must teach the student "html and css"  before moving to backend. The language that will be skip at frontend is javascript, Jqwidget and Template generators.Reason why we allow this is because some persons maight have gotten knowledge of frontend base on self tought and deceide to go over to backend.  We strongly recomment stiudent to finish frontend before moving to backend. 

 Can student include frontend training after completing backend?

Yes, student can return back to frontend after completing backend. At this point, Javascript must be handled at the frontend.

 Can new student jump in-between the section of the progress listed above

We only allow student who have knowledge on the  previous stage(s) of the progress to move to down stage of the progress.

 Do we teach web hosting to student who registered only for frontend.

Yes, We training student on how to host their FrontEnd website after they have completed the website.

 What is the duration at Fontend

The period of training depends on the regularity of the student. In our standard time of 3 days per week, the duration will be 2 months plus.  Due to some level of understanding of individual, sometimes it may take Maximum of 3 months. But if student is attending training every day, it will take 2 months to complete. Sundays excluded.
